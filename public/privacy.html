Privacy Policy

Effective: February 2026

Who We Are

Newphoria News is operated by Agapē LAC Inc., based in Calgary, Alberta, Canada. We curate and classify constructive news using artificial intelligence.

What We Collect

We collect minimal data to operate the service:

  • Email address — only if you subscribe to our newsletter or Newphoria+ premium service
  • Usage analytics — anonymous page views and interaction data to understand which content resonates
  • Payment information — processed securely through Stripe if you subscribe to Newphoria+. We do not store credit card numbers.

Cookies and Advertising

We use cookies for:

  • Essential cookies — required for the site to function
  • Analytics cookies — to understand traffic patterns (Google Analytics)
  • Advertising cookies — our advertising partners (including Google AdSense) may use cookies to serve relevant ads

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense — display advertising
  • Stripe — payment processing for premium subscriptions
  • Supabase — database hosting
  • Cloudflare — DNS and content delivery
  • Netlify — website hosting

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Cancel your premium subscription at any time

Data Retention

We retain your email address for as long as your subscription is active. If you unsubscribe, we delete your email within 30 days. Anonymous analytics data is retained indefinitely.

Children's Privacy

Newphoria is a general audience news platform. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
