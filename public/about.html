About Newphoria

Every moment of every day, somewhere on this planet, something extraordinary is happening. A breakthrough. An act of kindness. A discovery that rewrites what we thought we knew. Newphoria finds it.

Newphoria is an AI-curated news platform focused on constructive journalism — the stories of innovation, progress, science, and human achievement that traditional news feeds bury under doom and conflict.

We don't ignore reality. We rebalance it. The world has real problems, and they deserve attention. But the world also has millions of people solving those problems every single day, and those stories deserve attention too.

How It Works

Our AI engine continuously scans hundreds of news sources — from Reuters and NASA to The Lancet and community networks — and evaluates every article on a Bloom Scale of 1 to 5. Articles scoring 3 or above appear on Newphoria. Everything else gets filtered out.

The Bloom Score measures constructive value: Does this article inform? Does it point toward solutions? Does it show progress? Articles that focus purely on fear, conflict, or sensationalism score low and don't make it to your feed.

We also maintain a section called The Wonderfully Weird — for the genuinely strange and fascinating stories that don't fit neatly anywhere else. Unexplained phenomena, bizarre animal behavior, quantum strangeness, and the kind of stories that make you stop and think about how extraordinary this universe actually is.

The Technology

Newphoria is powered by the Coaptō document intelligence engine — the same AI platform designed to process and understand complex documents across industries. For Newphoria, the engine reads news articles instead of contracts and invoices, but the underlying pattern is identical: interpret unstructured content, classify it, and connect it into a structured, searchable system.

Who We Are
